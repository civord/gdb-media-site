About

One crew, start to finish.

Fernstone Landscapes is a Malvern East–based landscaping and concrete outfit — the kind of business that grew an audience the honest way, by posting the actual work. Over 2,300 people follow along on Instagram, watching gardens go from cleared dirt to finished spaces in real time.

The name behind the account keeps a simple rule: an 8am start, every job. No vague timelines, no chasing up — the crew turns up and gets into it.

From full garden builds to concrete paving and everything that connects them, the work is handled by one team, not handed off between subcontractors.

What we do

Landscaping and concrete,
under one crew.

Garden design & construction

Full landscape builds — garden beds, planting, structure — designed around how the space actually gets used.

02

Concrete & paving

Driveways, paths and slabs poured and finished properly — the foundation work most gardens are let down by.

03

Retaining walls

Sloped or uneven blocks squared away with retaining structures built to hold, not just look tidy for a season.

04

Turf & garden beds

New turf and planted beds laid out to fit the block, the light, and how much upkeep you actually want to do.

05

Outdoor entertaining areas

Patios, seating areas and the concrete or paved base underneath them, built as one connected project.

06

Site clean-ups & regrading

Overgrown or neglected blocks cleared and levelled — the unglamorous first step most gardens actually need.
