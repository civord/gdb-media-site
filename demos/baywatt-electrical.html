About

Local, licensed, and easy to reach on the phone.

Baywatt Electrical is run by Dean, a licensed electrician based on Griffin Crescent in Port Melbourne. The business covers the everyday electrical work this pocket of Melbourne actually needs — old switchboards in weatherboard cottages, extra power points in renovated warehouse apartments, and the growing list of homes adding EV chargers and split-system air con.

There's no dispatch centre and no apprentice sent out to figure it out — you deal directly with Dean, from the first call to the invoice. Jobs are quoted plainly before any work starts, and the site's left clean when it's done.

If it needs a licensed electrician's sign-off in Victoria, it's the kind of job this business takes on.

What Dean fixes

Electrical work, done to the standard,
not just done fast.

From a single dead power point to a full switchboard upgrade — every job is tested and left compliant with AS/NZS wiring rules before Dean drives off.

Switchboard upgrades

Old fuse boxes replaced with a modern, safety-switch-protected board — essential for period homes with original wiring.

Power points & wiring

New power points, USB outlets and general wiring for renovations, extra appliances or a rearranged room.

Safety switches & RCDs

Installation and testing of safety switches so faults trip the circuit, not the people in the house.

Hot water systems

Electrical connection and fault repair for hot water units — from a tripped element to a full swap.

EV charger installation

Home EV charger wiring sized correctly for the circuit — increasingly common across Port Melbourne driveways and garages.

Fault finding & repairs

Tripping circuits, flickering lights or dead outlets diagnosed and fixed — not just patched.

Ceiling fans & lighting

Ceiling fan installs, LED upgrades and outdoor lighting, wired properly the first time.

Rewiring & renovations

Partial or full rewires for older Port Melbourne homes, coordinated around your build timeline.
