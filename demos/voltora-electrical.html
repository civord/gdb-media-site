About

One electrician. Every job. No handoffs.

Voltora Electrical is Nick — a licensed electrician based in Brunswick West, working across Melbourne's inner north. The reviews tell the story better than any pitch could: patient with questions, reasonably priced, and the kind of finish that doesn't leave a mess behind.
