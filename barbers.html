How much does a barbershop website cost?

One fixed price up front, GST included, no lock-in — a one-chair studio and a six-chair shop with walk-ins need different sites, so you get a quote built around yours. Get a free quote and you'll know your exact number before any work starts.

Yes. We wire the "Book Now" button straight to whatever system you already use, so clients book themselves in — even at midnight — while you stay mid-fade. If you don't have one yet, we'll set up a simple booking-request flow.
