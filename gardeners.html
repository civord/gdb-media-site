Common Questions

Straight answers, no fine print.

How much does a landscaping website cost?

One fixed price up front, GST included, no lock-in — based on what your business needs. A solo mowing round and a full landscape construction crew need different sites, so you get a quote built around yours, not a menu price. Get a free quote.

Can you build my before-and-after galleries?

They're the centrepiece. Landscaping is a visual business — one muddy-yard-to-entertaining-area transformation sells harder than any paragraph. We structure your project photos into fast-loading galleries organised by project type, and show you exactly what to shoot on your phone.

Will my site rank across the seasons?

Garden searches come in bursts — spring clean-ups, summer irrigation, autumn planting, winter hardscaping. We build service content that catches every season, not just the one your site launched in, so the enquiries stay steady year-round.

Can you turn my Instagram following into enquiries?

That's the gap most landscapers have. Instagram gets you admired; a website gets you hired. We connect the two so followers land on a site that converts them into quote requests, with photo-upload forms so they can send you their yard.

Do you build suburb pages for my service area?

Yes. Clear service-area and suburb content tells Google exactly where you work, so you rank for "[service] + [suburb]" across your whole patch — and it's the structure that lets you expand into new areas later without rebuilding.
